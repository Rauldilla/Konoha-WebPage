Ichiraku Ramen

Ichiraku Ramen Shop is a small restaurant in Konoha that is runned bu Teuchi and his daughter Ayame .Like any ramen establishment, Ramen Ichiraku offers various toppings, such as char siu, boiled eggs, narutomaki, menma and seaweed. This restaurant sells the best ramen in Konoha. Come and enjoy a nice bowl of ramen! Try our most special bowl "Hokage tonkotsu miso ramen"

Hot Springs

The Konoha's Hot Springs is a public bath of Konohagakure, divided down the middle between genders. Shinobi can use the baths as a training ground for basic chakra training. Using the hot water as a form of motivation, the trainee is more focused in using their chakra to support themselves on the water. This is the best place to rest and relax in Konoha!

The academy

he Academy is a huge building in Konohagakure located directly at the base of the Hokage Mountain. It is where prospective ninja are trained and where official ninja receive their assignments. he building can be identified by the tree in front of it which has a swing on it and more so, by the giant sign with the kanji for "fire" (火) on it.
